# Retford.info - Site Walkthrough & Features

This document describes what users will see and how the site works.

## 🏠 Homepage

**What visitors see:**

### Header Section
- Light blue navbar with "R" logo and "Retford.info" title
- Mobile menu button (hamburger)
- Desktop menu with links to main categories
- Search bar (functional placeholder)

### Cover Section
- Large banner image with gradient background
- "Welcome to Retford" H1 heading
- Social sharing buttons (Facebook, Twitter)

### Introduction Section
- H2 "Your Local Community Hub" heading
- Description of Retford.info
- Logo placeholder with intro text

### Categories Grid (3 columns on desktop, 1 on mobile)
6 category cards with emojis:
- 🏪 Shops and Businesses
- 🍽️ Eat and Drink
- 🏨 Accommodation
- 🎭 Things to Do
- 📰 Latest News
- 📝 Latest Articles

Each card is clickable and links to that category page.

### Where is Retford Section
- H2 heading
- Description of Retford's location
- Map placeholder on right (2-column layout)
- Text describes geography and benefits

### About Retford.info
- H2 heading
- Description of the platform
- Light gray background section

### Footer
- 4-column layout:
  1. Logo + Copyright
  2. Information links (Privacy, Terms, Contact, About)
  3. Category links
  4. "Advertise Your Business" section (light blue box)

## Colors & Styling

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Black text on white background
- **Accent:** Light blue (#87CEEB) - used for:
  - Buttons
  - Links (underlined)
  - Navbar background
  - Featured business cards
  - Featured badges
  - Important headings
- **Accent Dark:** Darker blue (#4A90E2) - used for:
  - Hover states
  - Dark backgrounds
  - Dark text on accent background
- **Gray:** Various grays for:
  - Borders
  - Background sections
  - Less important text

### Typography
- Clean sans-serif font
- Large, readable headings
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3, etc.)
- Underlined links (in accent color)
- No animations or transitions (as specified)
- Mobile-optimized font sizes

### Responsive Design
- **Mobile (<768px):** Single column, larger touch targets
- **Tablet (768-1024px):** 2 columns where appropriate
- **Desktop (1024px+):** Full layouts, 3-4 columns

## User Journeys

### Visitor Looking for a Business

1. Lands on homepage
2. Sees category grid
3. Clicks category (e.g., "Eat and Drink")
4. Sees featured businesses first
5. Scrolls to see all businesses
6. Clicks business name
7. Sees full details, hours, contact info
8. Clicks to contact or visit website

### Business Owner Looking to List

1. Lands on homepage
2. Clicks "Advertise" in footer (or navbar)
3. Sees pricing comparison
4. Clicks "Get Listed" button
5. Goes to contact page
6. Fills out contact form
7. Receives response with listing details

### Visitor Looking for News/Info

1. Lands on homepage
2. Clicks "Latest News" or "Latest Articles"
3. Sees list of posts
4. Clicks one to read
5. Reads article
6. Sees related content
7. Clicks related article
8. Continues exploring
