# Retford Celebrates Community Week

This week, Retford is buzzing with community spirit as we celebrate what makes our town so special. From market stalls to live entertainment, there's something for everyone.

## Events This Week

- **Monday**: Local businesses showcase event
- **Tuesday**: Community market with local producers
- **Wednesday**: Live music in the town square
- **Thursday**: Family fun day at the leisure centre
- **Friday**: Special shopping evening with discounts
- **Saturday**: Street fair and festival
- **Sunday**: Community picnic in the park

## Get Involved

We encourage all residents and visitors to get involved and support our local community. Whether it's visiting local shops, trying new restaurants, or simply enjoying the atmosphere, every bit helps support our wonderful town.

## More Information

For full details of all events, visit the town centre notice board or ask at any local business.
