# Welcome to Retford

Retford is a vibrant market town in the heart of Nottinghamshire, with a rich history spanning over a thousand years. Whether you're a long-time resident or visiting for the first time, there's so much to discover in our wonderful community.

## A Town with Character

From our historic market square to the beautiful River Idle, Retford offers a unique blend of traditional charm and modern amenities. Our town centre is home to independent shops, quality restaurants, and traditional pubs that have served the community for generations.

## What's On Offer

Whether you're looking to explore local attractions, enjoy a meal out, find accommodation, or simply soak up the atmosphere of a proper English market town, Retford has something for everyone.
